SOP: IT & Infrastructure — Systems Management

Purpose

To define procedures for managing the hospital’s IT systems, ensuring data integrity, cybersecurity, system uptime, and user support operations.

Scope

Applies to all IT staff, system administrators, and end users accessing hospital systems such as EMR, PACS, HR, and internal networks.

Responsibilities

  • IT Manager: Oversee IT infrastructure, enforce security policies, and approve system changes.
  • System Administrators: Implement updates, monitor servers, and ensure daily backups.
  • Helpdesk Staff: Log incidents, assign tickets, and provide user support within SLA timelines.

Procedures

  1. User Accounts: Requests are submitted via an IT service ticket and approved by department heads. Inactive accounts are disabled after 30 days.
  2. Backup & Recovery: Perform daily incremental backups and weekly full backups. Store offsite copies and test recovery quarterly.
  3. System Maintenance: Apply updates during scheduled maintenance (Sunday 2 AM–4 AM). Notify users in advance.
  4. Incident Management: Log issues in the IT ticketing system; critical incidents resolved within 2 hours.

Service Levels

  • Critical system uptime target: 99.95%
  • Ticket response time: 30 minutes
  • Backup success rate: 100%
