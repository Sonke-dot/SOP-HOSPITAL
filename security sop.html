SOP: Hospital Security

Purpose

This SOP outlines the policies and procedures that ensure the safety of patients, staff, and visitors within hospital facilities. It aims to prevent unauthorized access, manage security incidents, and maintain a secure environment 24/7.

Scope

This SOP applies to all hospital staff, contractors, visitors, and security personnel across all buildings, parking areas, and external premises.

Responsibilities

  • Security Manager: Oversees all security operations, incident investigations, and compliance audits.
  • Security Officers: Conduct regular patrols, monitor CCTV, and respond to alerts and emergencies.
  • Reception Staff: Verify visitors, issue access passes, and maintain the visitor logbook.
  • All Staff: Display ID badges and report any suspicious activities immediately.

Procedures

  1. Access Control: All employees must use valid ID cards to enter secure zones. Lost cards must be reported immediately for deactivation.
  2. Visitor Management: Visitors must sign in, present identification, and be escorted by authorized personnel when required.
  3. Surveillance & Patrols: Security officers conduct hourly patrols and review CCTV feeds for irregular activity.
  4. Incident Reporting: Any theft, violence, or suspicious behavior must be reported within 30 minutes using the Incident Report Form (SEC-01).
  5. Lockdown & Evacuation: During a threat or critical incident, activate the hospital lockdown protocol and coordinate with the Emergency Response Team.
